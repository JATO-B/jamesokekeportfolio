About Me

Hi, I'm James Okeke, a cybersecurity enthusiast committed to continuous growth, with hands-on experience in networking, vulnerability assessment, penetration testing, digital forensics, and defensive security. My journey in cybersecurity began through formal training with Digital Skills Africa and The Incubator Hub, where I completed practical projects that strengthened my technical skills and passion for protecting systems.

My initial exposure to technology came through frontend development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which sparked my curiosity about how systems function beneath the surface. Later, as a teacher and Vice Principal, I managed digital exam platforms such as Brainfriend, EDVES, and Klacify, experiences that enhanced my problem-solving skills and introduced me to IT systems management.

Beyond technology, I have also served as a music tutor and choirmaster, guiding choristers and developing leadership, discipline, and effective communication skills, qualities that I now bring into my work in cybersecurity.

I'm a continuous learner, committed to building a career in ethical hacking and network security. This portfolio showcases my growth, projects, and the skills I continue to develop along the way. Thanks for stopping by!
