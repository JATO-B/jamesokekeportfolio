About Me

Hi, I'm James Okeke, a cybersecurity enthusiast committed to continuous growth. My journey into cybersecurity began in 2025, and it's been an exciting ride so far, thanks to the hands-on training I received from Digital Skill Africa (DSA) and The Incubator Hub. Over the past four months, their practical, project-based approach opened my eyes to what’s possible in this field.

I started out with foundational knowledge in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which sparked my interest in how the web functions, from surface interactions to the underlying systems that keep it secure. This early exposure laid the groundwork for my transition into cybersecurity, where I now focus on understanding system vulnerabilities and how to defend against them.

I'm especially drawn to ethical hacking and network security, and I’m actively building my career in this space through continuous learning, experimentation, and real-world projects.

This portfolio is where I document and share my growth, experiences, and the things I build along the way. Thanks for stopping by!
